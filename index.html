FREQUENTLY ASKED

Who is the workshop for?

Middle-school, high-school, college-age students and young adults. Content is tailored to your group's age and goals.

Is it available in Spanish?

Yes — the Hero C.O.D.E. can be delivered fully in English, Spanish or bilingual.

In person or virtual?

Both. Clarissa presents on-site for schools and groups, or remotely for distributed audiences.

What qualifies Clarissa to speak to youth?

Clarissa is an attorney and former cabinet-level agency head who led a 1,000-person New York State agency, was appointed by two NY Governors and twice confirmed by the State Senate, and is admitted to the U.S. Supreme Court Bar. A bilingual keynote speaker and mentor with the Governor's NYS Latina Mentoring Initiative, she began her career as a community organizer in the Bronx and Brooklyn — and she's a proud mom of two beautiful daughters. Read her full bio →

How much does it cost?

The workshop is free. In return, Clarissa appreciates your feedback on how to improve the program — and any other feedback you'd like to share.

Does Clarissa also do workshops or talks for adults?

Yes — this workshop was actually adapted from a similar talk she gives to executives, non-profit organizations, government leaders, managers and others interested in leadership topics. Please contact her for more information.
